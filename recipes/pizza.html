Pepperoni pizza

Italian dish typically consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients,baked at high temperature, traditionally in a wood fired oven.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of while flour
  • 1 spoon of yeast
  • 1 spoon of sugar
  • 2/3 cup of warm water + 2 spoons
  • 2 sprays of oil
  • 1 cup of sauce
  • 6 ounces of mozzarella cheese

How do I make it?

  1. Wash your hands
  2. Preheat your furnace at 375F
  3. Collect all ingredients
  4. In a medium sized bowl, mix 1 cup of flour, the yeast, the sugar and the water using a spoon
  5. Start mixing the other cup of flour until you get a dough ball
  6. Get some flour into a cutting board and play knead the ball for 5 minutes
  7. Spray a bowl with your oil spray. Place the dough into it and cover it with something clean. Let it sit for 15 minutes
  8. Get something to bake the dough with and spray it with oil. Extend the dough over the surface you got to bake it
  9. Add a layer of sauce leaving uncovered a bit of the edge
  10. Add cheese and your other toppings to the pizza
  11. Bake for around 15 minutes until the cheese is toasted on some parts
  12. Enjoy!
